Our Latest Events

GCS- Global Climate Strike

Gorakhpur Pant Park

Global Climate Strike is a powerful movement demanding urgent action to address the escalating climate crisis. By mobilizing people worldwide to participate in strikes, protests, and advocacy efforts, it aims to raise awareness, pressure governments and corporations to implement effective policies.

Cleanliness and Awareness Drive

Gorakhpur Nouka vihar

The Cleanliness and Awareness Drive is a community-driven initiative aimed at promoting hygiene, environmental stewardship, and education on sustainable practices. By organizing clean-up activities.

Chalk of Shame

Gorakhpur Nouka vihar

"Chalk of Shame" refers to a creative and impactful method of drawing attention to littering and environmental negligence. By using chalk to outline discarded items or areas affected by pollution.

Plantation Drive

Gorakhpur Bajaj Park

The Plantation Drive is a concerted effort to enhance our environment by planting trees and nurturing green spaces. It aims to combat deforestation, mitigate climate change, and promote biodiversity. By mobilizing communities, organizations, and volunteers.

3R's(Reduse, Reuse, Recycle)

Gorakhpur Water Complex

The 3R's (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) embody a holistic approach to sustainable living and environmental stewardship. By reducing consumption and minimizing waste generation, we can lessen our environmental footprint and conserve precious resources. Through the reuse of items, we extend their lifespan and minimize the need for new production, thereby reducing both waste and resource consumption.

Queer support Awerness

Gorakhpur Water Complex

The Queer Support Awareness initiative is dedicated to fostering inclusivity, understanding, and advocacy for the LGBTQ+ community. Through education, outreach, and support networks, this initiative aims to raise awareness about the challenges faced by queer individuals and promote acceptance and equality for all.
